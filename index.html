BVSS Maratha Students Home is a venerable institution that traces its roots back to its founding by B. Venkata Rao and S. Sajjan Rao in the year 1909. This distinguished hostel holds a rich legacy of providing unwavering support and accommodation to students from the Maratha community. Committed to fostering educational opportunities, BVSS Maratha Students Home stands as a free hostel dedicated to nurturing the academic aspirations of Maratha students. With a century-long tradition of excellence, the hostel remains a steadfast abode that not only offers lodging but also cultivates a nurturing environment conducive to learning and personal growth. At BVSS Maratha Students Home, we take pride in our commitment to the educational well-being of our residents, empowering them to strive for success and contribute meaningfully to society.
